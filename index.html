Tetris The Grand Master 1 - Controls

Move Left: Z
Move Right: C
Soft Drop: S
Rotate Clockwise: K
Rotate Counter-Clockwise: SPACE or L
Hard Drop: X
Pause: ESC
Restart (Game Over): ENTER

Goal: Reach level 999 with the highest grade possible!
Grades are earned based on score thresholds. Grand Master requires reaching level 999 with 126,000+ points in under 13:30.
